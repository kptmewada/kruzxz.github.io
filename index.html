FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we work together.

What services do you offer? +
I offer full-stack web development (React, Node.js, Python), cyber security consulting and penetration testing, and cloud/DevOps infrastructure engineering. I can work as an embedded team member or deliver fixed-scope projects.
How quickly can you start? +
Most projects kick off within 3–5 days after the initial scoping call. I'll provide a detailed proposal within 48 hours of our first conversation.
Do you work with non-technical founders? +
Absolutely. I translate technical complexity into plain language. You'll always know exactly what's being built, why, and when — with weekly demos and clear milestone sign-offs.
What does a security audit involve? +
A typical security engagement covers reconnaissance, vulnerability scanning, manual testing (OWASP Top 10, business logic flaws, auth weaknesses), and a written report with severity ratings and step-by-step remediation guidance. I can also assist with the fix implementation.
What if the project isn't what I expected? +
Projects are milestone-gated — you review and approve each phase before work continues. If at the first milestone you're unhappy, you owe nothing. No lock-in, no termination fees.
Do you sign NDAs? +
Yes. I'm happy to sign an NDA before any scoping discussions. Confidentiality is standard practice for all client engagements.
